What is the Ethics & AI Committee?

The Ethics & AI Committee is a collaborative forum where members explore ethical dilemmas, discuss best practices, and help shape responsible AI development and usage.

Committee members participate in structured debates, review case studies, and contribute to quarterly transparency reports that help improve Biasbuster and advocate for responsible AI in society.

Whether you're a student, educator, AI professional, or concerned citizen, your perspective matters in this ongoing dialogue about the ethical dimensions of artificial intelligence.
