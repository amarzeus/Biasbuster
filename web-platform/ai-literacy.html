How AI Detects Bias

Our advanced AI system analyzes text using multiple parameters:

  • Language patterns and word choice
  • Sentiment analysis
  • Context evaluation
  • Source credibility assessment

Types of Bias

Confirmation Bias

Tendency to favor information that confirms existing beliefs.

Selection Bias

Cherry-picking data or stories that support a particular narrative.

Attribution Bias

Attributing behaviors to character rather than circumstance.

AI Limitations
